Incoming Freshman Admission Requirements


  • Original Report Card (Form 138)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Certified True Copy of Birth Certificate (NSO)
  • Two (2) pieces of recent ID picture, 2"x2" colored
  • Long brown envelope


Transferee Admission Requirements


  • Original copy of the official Honorable Dismissal
  • Transcript of Records or Certification of Grades from the last school attended
  • Certified True Copy of Birth Certificate (NSO)
  • Two (2) pieces of recent ID picture, 2"x2" colored
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Long brown envelope


Returning Students (Re-admission)


  • Returning students must report to the registrar office to process the necessary clearance and documentation before they are allowed to enroll.
